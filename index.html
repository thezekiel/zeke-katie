Where to stay

Renting out an empty apartment through Airbnb is a fantastic alternative to hotels in NYC. There are usually lots of great options available in Brooklyn in the Summer, often cheaper and more convenient than hotels. The neighborhoods we recommend are Greenpoint, Williamsburg, Brooklyn Heights, Cobble Hill, SOHO, West Village or East Village.

If you have a large-ish group, VRBO (HomeAway) is another great option for renting out larger houses and apartments throughout the city. This will also likely be cheaper and more comfortable than a hotel.

Getting Around

NYC yellow cabs and green cabs work great – you just need to flag them down. They all take credit cards, and most trips between our venues in Brooklyn will cost in the $10 - $18 range. A cab from JFK or LaGuardia airport will cost about $60 to most places in Manhattan and Brooklyn (Newark airport will be a bit more expensive via cab).

Another good option is using Uber or Lyft car services. These usually cost similar to a normal cab, but they will come right to you if you can’t find a cab. We suggest downloading the apps and setting up an account ahead of time.
