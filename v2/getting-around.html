Cabs, Trains, & Uber-mobiles

NYC yellow cabs and green cabs are great – you just need to flag them down. They all take credit cards, and most trips between the weekend’s venues in Brooklyn will cost around $10 – $20. A cab from JFK or LaGuardia airport will cost about $60 to most places in Manhattan and Brooklyn (Newark airport will be a bit more expensive via cab).

Another good option is Uber or Lyft car services. The cost is similar to a normal cab, but you can request a car to come directly to you, and track its location through the app. We suggest downloading the apps and setting up an account ahead of time.

NYC’s subway system, the MTA, is great. Each ride is $2.75, and you can buy cards in the station vending machines. We suggest filling it with a dollar amount if you’re planning on sharing the card. A 7-day unlimited option may be a better deal if you’re riding the train a lot, but you’ll have to wait 20 min between swipes (not so helpful for sharing).The lines and stops that are most relevant to our wedding locations are:
